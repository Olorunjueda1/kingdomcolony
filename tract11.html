YOUR DREAMS AND YOU

Naturally, no living human being will sleep and wake up without dreaming. Some of you may say no, but know for sure that you dream whenever you sleep it is possible that you may wake up and forget them. This alone is a problem which you need to attack spiritually and overcome because, forgetfulness of dreams can make you to remain in the cage of your enemies for life.

The reason why you dream is because God wants you to know yourself. You should know that you are more of the spirit than physical.

Your spirit-man may not be active when your flesh is active. But when your flesh is inactive through sleep your spirit being now becomes very sensitive. That is why the wicked normally conjure or manipulates your spirit when you are asleep. And if they do these things or more to you and you wake up without remembering, how will you counter their works?

Idiomatically, the grammarians will say that your dream is your vision, goal or aspiration of what you will be in life.

Many today could not see their vision and aspiration of greatness fulfilled; at last they end up where they do not bargain for.

Your goal and objective in life may be your dream and vision indeed, but if this is not achieved right from the spirit, you may never and will never achieve it in the physical.

You must not joke with your dream life. It is one of the mediums through which God and Satan talk to people. This is the more reason why you need to move closer to God through His word, so that every dream may be established in the Bible.

Have you ever dreamt that your head was cut off and was replaced with another? Have you seen yourself carrying a baby on your back in your dream? Have you dreamt that your spectacle was taken in the dream? Do you normally see yourself playing with snakes?

Have you been seeing yourself naked or walking bare footed in your dream?

As a married man, have you dreamt that your covering cloth was taken or rent into two or more?

Are you fond of eating and drinking in your dream? Have you seen your hair been cut away in your dream?

Look! You can never be greater than your dream, some of you as Christians find it difficult to live a holy life. Why? Because many a time you see yourself marching excreta or falling upon it, you see yourself at times where they are throwing garbage. How will you be free from immoral thought and act?

I am calling you to serious prayer and deliverance if you have known Jesus and accepted Him as Lord and Saviour. If not, you need to first receive Jesus into your life and as you pray he will rescue you.
