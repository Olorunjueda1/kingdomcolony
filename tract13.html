FREEDOM FROM SPIRITUAL PRISON

Who will cut the rope tied round the bird’s leg?

Who will loosen the rope from the tree the bird is tied to?

Who will deliver the suffering bird from its cage?

Who will provide the bird water to drink and food to eat? Can food & water provided while still in bondage be taken with joy, peace and freedom?

Who will liberate the spiritually imprisoned fellow who does not know that the physical is controlled by the spiritual?

The fowler of men is Satan, many birds are in his bandage either by being tied down with rope or by being caged in a cage. Who will deliver the prey from the mouth of the lion?

Who will set free the captive of the mighty?

How can the captive of the mighty be set free without knowing that he is in captivity?

How can the prey of the lion escape from the lion’s mouth when he knows not that he is grazing on a dangerous field? How can a birds escape the cage of the fowler when, it knows not that the food kept for him is a bait to catch him?

Brethren, do you experience the following in your life? If yes, you are in the net of Satan already.

Why are you not zealous as you were when you first believed?

  1. Continuous failure.
  2. Continuous sinning habit, sinning thought.
  3. An hindrance to success or achievement.
  4. Pirouette or rotating at one edge for long.
  5. Persistent problem without improvement or success.
  6. Laboring hard but getting little or nothing to show for it.
  7. Vicious evil circle from time to time.
  8. Unclear vision as a result of thick cloud.
  9. Laboring physically in the day and fear of nightmare and dreadful dream in the night.
  10. Serving God with lack of freedom from lust, unholy thought.
  11. Going into compromise with enemies of God (Occultism).
  12. Affliction through little meat, food, milk etc.
  13. Continuous or periodic sickness.
  14. Abnormal experience (late marriage, untimely death etc.)
  15. Evil attraction nearly all the time.
  16. Bad dream all the time e.g.
  • swimming in the water
  • playing by the riverside
  • cutting grass and growing after you
  • seeing opposite sex making love with you
  • menstruating in the dream
  • becoming pregnant or breast feeding in the dream
  • wedded physically but seen yourself wedding in the dream again
  • packing snail in the dream
  • walking aimlessly in the dream
  • seeing some of your valuable thing stolen or voluntarily given to people
  • seeing masquerade in the dream
  • seeing dead people in the dream etc.

Any of the above and many unmentioned, tells you that you are in the bondage of the fowler of men.

Here is the good news; Jesus is the head of all principalities. He has taken captivity captive and has given gifts of freedom, salvation, deliverance, holiness, peace unto men Eph 4:8 He said in Isaiah chapter 49: 24-25 that “ shall prey be taking from mighty or the lawful captive delivered? But thus saith the Lord, even the captives of the mighty shall be taken away, and the prey of the terrible shall be delivered”. Even though you sold yourself to him yet God said he will deliver you. Now the ball is in your court. All you need to do now is to play right to goal post by confessing your known sin, renouncing and forsaking them by accepting Jesus as your Lord and Saviour. Then he will accept you, free you from the bandage of Satan and give you divine sonship identity card by being a joint heir with Him.
