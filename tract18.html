KNOWLEDGE AND YOU

Knowledge is powers

Knowledge delivers

Knowledge helps

Through knowledge, you are informed for you not to be deformed. Have you knowledge of your environment? Have you the true knowledge of who you are? Do you know that you can never die? Do you know that your body is just your house on this earth? And it is when you are out of your house, the people will concludes that you are dead? Look! You can never die when people died it is just a transition to your final and everlasting abode.

Do you know that the final abode for you is made up of two places? Do you know that one of those places is not originally created for man but for Satan and his fallen angels? Do you know that the majority of those that have left their terrestrial house are now in hell? Why? Just because they refused to eat the fruit of life that God gave to man. Do you know why you are dying daily to holiness, righteousness and love of God? This is as a result of fruit of knowledge of good and of evil that you are eating through, what you see in your society, what you hear that edify not, what you browse on your computer etc.

Do you know that you can shift from eating the fruit of knowledge of good and of evil to eating the fruit of life? Jesus says He is the bread of life. If you can be living on the fruit of life if you turn to Jesus today. Jesus gave Himself to you for your food “I am the living bread which came down from heaven: if any man eat of this bread, he shall live for ever…” John 6:51 “Verily verily I say unto you, except ye eat the flesh of the son of man, and drinks his blood, ye have no life in you” John 6:53 man have lost his life in the garden of Eden that is why man is evil is evil on earth, that is why his imaginations and thoughts are evil; which will cost him eternal separation from God at the end of his life on earth. But through Jesus flesh and blood, you have your life back, you have your life of holiness, righteousness and genuine love of God back which will earn you eternal life with God back which will earn you eternal life with God. For God is not the God of the devil but of the living.

Therefore friend, accept Jesus as your lord and savior by confessing and forsaken your known and unknown sin and so, you will regain back your life.

Your acceptance of Jesus will qualify you for the fruit of life in Him.

Welcome to God’s family.
